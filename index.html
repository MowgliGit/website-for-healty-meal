Healthy chilli con carne

Heat the oil in a large heavybased pan over medium-high heat. Add onion and celery, and cook, stirring, for 1-2 minutes. Add mince and cook, breaking up any large pieces with a wooden spoon, for 4 minutes Add the spice mix and cumin. Season well.

Stir in lentils and tomato. Add 375ml (1Â½ cups) water. Bring mixture to the boil. Reduce heat and simmer, uncovered, for 20 minutes or until the lentils are cooked and mixture has thickened.

Stir through the capsicum and beans, and cook for 1-2 minutes. Stir through half the coriander and half the chilli. Divide chilli con carne among serving bowls. Scatter with the remaining coriander and chilli.

Ricotta maple peaches

Preheat oven to 180C. Line a large baking tray with baking paper. Lightly brush a barbecue grill or chargrill with a little oil. Heat on medium-high. Lightly brush the peach halves, cut-side up, with the maple syrup. Cook on grill for 2 mins or until lightly charred.

Transfer the peaches to the lined tray. Sprinkle with rosemary and thyme sprigs and season with pepper. Drizzle with a little oil. Bake for 5-7 mins or until just tender and heated through.

Green salad with pickled grapes

Combine vinegar, oil and sugar in a bowl. Add grapes and onion. Stir to combine. Set aside for 10 minutes.

Combine lettuces and watercress in a serving bowl. Top with cucumber, avocado and radish. Spoon over grape mixture. Sprinkle with almonds. Season with salt and pepper. Serve.
